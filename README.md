# Account Deletion – Business Analysis Case Study

## Overview
The Account Deletion feature enables customers to request the removal of their account and all associated personal data from the platform. This functionality is designed to provide a secure, transparent, and user-friendly offboarding experience while ensuring that deletion requests are properly managed and processed by the system.

The feature supports both customer-facing interactions and internal administrative operations. From the user perspective, it includes a structured deletion request flow with confirmation steps to prevent accidental account removal, as well as optional feedback collection during the offboarding process. Users are also kept informed through communication updates regarding the status of their deletion request.

From a business and operational perspective, the feature provides administrators with the necessary tools to manage, track, and process account deletion requests efficiently. This ensures alignment with data handling policies, operational workflows, and regulatory requirements.

## Objective
To define clear and structured requirements for allowing users to permanently delete their accounts while ensuring security, compliance, and user experience.

## Scope
- User-initiated account deletion
- Confirmation and verification steps
- Data handling after deletion
